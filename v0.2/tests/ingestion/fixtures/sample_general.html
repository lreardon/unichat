About Example University

Example University is a leading research institution founded in 1890. We are committed to excellence in teaching, research, and community engagement.

Our Mission

To advance knowledge and educate students in science, technology, and the humanities for the betterment of society.

Quick Facts

  • 25,000 students from 120 countries
  • 3,000 faculty members
  • Ranked #45 globally (QS World University Rankings 2026)
  • $500M annual research funding

Campus Locations

Our main campus is located in downtown Springfield, with satellite campuses in Shelbyville and Capital City.
