MSc Data Science

The MSc in Data Science is an interdisciplinary program combining statistics, computer science, and domain expertise. Duration: 18 months.

Core Courses

DSCI 501 - Statistical Learning
Foundations of supervised and unsupervised learning methods.
DSCI 502 - Data Engineering
Building data pipelines, ETL processes, and data warehousing.
DSCI 503 - Machine Learning Systems
Deploying ML models at scale: serving, monitoring, and MLOps.

PhD Programs

The School of Engineering offers PhD programs in all departments. Typical duration is 4-6 years. All PhD students receive full funding.
